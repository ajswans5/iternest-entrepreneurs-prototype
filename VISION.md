# IterNest for Entrepreneurs — Experience Vision

This repository is the living clickable prototype for **IterNest for Entrepreneurs**.

It is **not** the production app. It is the experience model Friday should use as the source of truth.

## Core feeling

The product should feel like sitting down at a warm coffee shop with a thoughtful founder coach.

Not a dashboard.  
Not a form.  
Not a productivity worksheet.  
Not a pile of analytics.

A place where an entrepreneur can say, "Here is what is tangled," and the app helps them find the next right move.

## Product promise

IterNest for Entrepreneurs helps founders turn messy thoughts into clear next decisions.

The app should reduce cognitive load, not add more.

## Design rule #1

If something does not help the founder make their next decision, it does not belong on the screen.

## Design principles

1. Conversation first. The coaching is the interface.
2. Warm over corporate.
3. Calm is not a tagline; it is a feeling created by restraint.
4. Less explanation on screen. Use reveal-on-demand for deeper reasoning.
5. The app should interpret the founder's blocker, not simply repeat it back.
6. Buttons should feel like useful next moves, not categories in a form.
7. The experience should preserve the engine underneath while simplifying what the user sees.
8. The prototype is the source of truth for layout, pacing, wording, and interaction feel.

## Brand language

Use **IterNest for Entrepreneurs** as the product title.

Avoid using FounderFlow as the public title in this prototype. FounderFlow may remain an internal engine concept, but the brand shown to users is IterNest.

## North star

The first screen should make the founder feel:

> I can sit down here, say the real thing, and find my next step.
